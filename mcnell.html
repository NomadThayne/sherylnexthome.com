About this property

Located on one of Ojai's most beloved streets, 757 McNell Road offers the quintessential Ojai lifestyle — close to trails, close to town, and surrounded by the natural beauty that makes the valley so special. This 3-bedroom, 2-bath home sits on a third of an acre with room to breathe and the quiet character of a true Ojai neighborhood.

McNell Road runs through the heart of the East End, one of Ojai's most sought-after areas, with oak-canopied streets, mountain views, and easy access to the Ojai Valley Trail. A wonderful opportunity to put down roots in the valley.
