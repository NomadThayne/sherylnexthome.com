Nature & Recreation

Outdoors & Trails

🚴
Paved Trail · Valley-wide
Ojai Valley Trail

A beloved 9.5-mile paved multi-use trail running through the entire valley — from Ojai to Ventura. Ideal for walking, cycling, and running. Shaded by oaks through much of its length.

Trail info ↗
🌲
National Forest
Los Padres National Forest

Over 1.75 million acres of wilderness bordering the Ojai Valley. Hiking, camping, and horseback riding. Trailheads accessible directly from the valley.

fs.usda.gov ↗
🎣
Reservoir · Recreation
Lake Casitas

A 6-mile-long reservoir west of Ojai. Fishing, camping, boating, and picnicking. Home to record-size largemouth bass. No swimming.

Phone (805) 649-2233
lakecasitas.info ↗

Transport & Distances

Getting Around

A car is strongly recommended. Ojai is a rural valley community. While public transit connects to Ventura, most errands and adventures require a vehicle. That said, the valley itself is very bikeable.
